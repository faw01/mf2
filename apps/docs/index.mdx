## Make it yours

1. Set your product name in `docs.json` (the `name` field).
2. Swap the logo and favicon in `logo/` and `favicon.svg`.
3. Write pages as MDX files and list them under `navigation` in `docs.json`.

See the [Mintlify documentation](https://mintlify.com/docs) for components, theming, and deployment.
